Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions we receive about our wedding. If you have any other questions, feel free to reach out!

General Questions

Q: When and where is the wedding?

A:We’re saying “I do” on 29 January 2026 at Avianto. The ceremony will take place in the chapel and starts promptly at 15:00. The celebration continues with a reception in the banquet hall, all at the same venue.

Q: What should I wear? Is there a dress code?

A: Yes! Our dress code is strictly formal, and our colour scheme is soft browns, muted greens, blush pinks and dusty blues. Please visit the “Attire” tab for some inspiration. Think elegant, polished, and sophisticated.

Q: Can I bring a guest?

A: We’re keeping our celebration intimate, so invitations are for named guests only. Thank you for understanding and helping us keep our day personal.

Wedding Details

Q: Is the ceremony indoors or outdoors?

A: The ceremony will be indoors, so you won’t have to worry about the weather.

Q: What time should I arrive?

A: We recommend arriving between 14:15 and 14:30 to allow time for parking and finding your seat before the ceremony begins at 14:00.

Q: Are kids welcome?

A: Children listed on family invitations are warmly welcome. Otherwise, we kindly ask for an adults-only celebration.

Gifts & Giving

Q: Do you have a gift registry?

A: Your presence truly is the best gift we could ask for. We don’t have a formal gift registry, but if you would like to contribute to our future together, we are gratefully accepting monetary gifts. Your kindness and support mean the world to us, thank you for being part of our story.

RSVP

Q: How do I RSVP?

A: Head to the RSVP tab, search your name and click ATTENDING if you will be blessing us with your presence.

Q: What if I have more questions?

A: Feel free to reach out directly to the Mother of the Bride, Melody, at 067 346 1924 or Mother of the Groom, Petula, 073 656 4723. They are happy to help with anything you’re unsure about.
